VOLCANOS

WHAT ARE VOLCANOS?

A volcano is a rupture in the crust of a planetary-mass object, such as Earth,that allows hot lava, volcanic ash, and gases to escape from a magma chamber below the surface.

MOUNTAINS

WHAT ARE MOUNTAINS?

A mountain is an elevated portion of the Earth's crust, generally with steep sides that show significant exposed bedrock.

CITIES

WHAT ARE CITIES?

A city is a large human settlement. A permanent and densely settled place with administratively defined boundaries whose members work primarily on non-agricultural tasks

WHAT ARE FLOWERS?

A flower, sometimes known as a bloom or blossom, is the reproductive structure found in flowering plants.
